About Us

Welcome to Creative Constructions, Srikakulam's trusted partner in building and development. For years, our family-run business has been dedicated to delivering high-quality residential and commercial projects throughout Andhra Pradesh. We combine modern techniques with timeless craftsmanship to bring our clients' visions to life.

Our commitment to excellence, integrity, and customer satisfaction is the foundation of every project we undertake. As a VUDA-licensed company, we are authorized to handle all plan approvals, ensuring a seamless, compliant, and transparent process for our valued clients.

Meet Our Managing Director: M Srinivasa Rao

M Srinivasa Rao leads Creative Constructions with a vision for quality and a passion for excellence. With decades of experience in the construction industry, he has been the guiding force behind our most successful projects.

His commitment to client satisfaction and innovative building solutions has established our reputation as a leader in Srikakulam's construction sector.

Meet Our Executive Director: M V S Sai Surya

M V S Sai Surya, as the Executive Director, brings dynamic leadership and fresh perspectives to Creative Constructions. With a strong focus on innovation and efficiency, he plays a key role in expanding our capabilities and ensuring cutting-edge project execution.

His expertise complements the Managing Director's seasoned vision, driving the company forward with modern strategies and unwavering dedication to client satisfaction.
